Welcome to Big Bug Warfare

The game where you fight over the control of New Bug City
take control of new areas like the Antagon or Pims pool to unluck new troops
use these different types of troops for example the Catasect or the Sharpsect
but be aware of the weather, if it storms you can be finished in a blink
or when it's foggy it's harder to hit your shots.
Strategies, attack, defeat and out smart your enemy to win the game!

The Colonies

The 4 colonies: The Ants, The Spiders, The Praying Mantis and the Mosquitos fight for the control of New Bug City.

The colonies recruit other bugs like ladybugs and beetles to help them defeat there enemy.

The Ants

The Ants are the citizens of New Bug City and want to start a revolution

They are done with the government underpaying them and want to take control and make a new authority

The Spiders

The Spiders are the mafia of New Bug City and the saw the recent revolution of the ants as an opportunity to take control of the city

By using there existing power they can over power the other colonies with ease.

The Praying Mantis

The Praying Mantis are the mercenaries of New Bug City they are sick of being ignored and are taking matters into their own hands

They want to control the economy of the city and execute anyone who doesn't obey their laws.

The Mosquitos

The Mosquitos are the police of New Bug City and are the defenders of the government and want to keep everything the same

They will use any means necessary to keep the city under control.
